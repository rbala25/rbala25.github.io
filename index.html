Hi. I'm Rishi Bala.

I'm a junior at Newark Academy interested in Engineering/Computer Science.

See my Resume

In the past couple of years I have pursued multiple different hobbies and leadership positions, learning and using multiple different programming languages including Java, JavaScript, and Python, while also teaching myself design softwares such as AutoCAD and Fusion360. I have further studied theoretical computer science and discrete mathematics through the Program in Algorithmic and Combinatorial Thinking, taught by Prof. Rajiv Gandhi, and I continue to teach younger students robotics hardware and block coding.

Outside of school, I spend much of time leading my VEX Robotics team, where I design, CAD, and physically build. As a team, we have won multiple awards, both locally in New Jersey and globally. In school, I am a part of numerous other groups. To name a few, I am a lead editor in both the school yearbook and newspaper, and I play for the boy's tennis team. Another hobby of mine is the jazz drums, and I currently am the lead drummer in the Newark Academy Big Band.
